## Search Options Menu

<br>

-   Press `Capslock` to open

    <br>

-   Press `Capslock` / `Tab` / `Esc` to close

    <br>

-   Press `A` to toggle `automatic suggestions`

    *Manual suggestions are shown / hidden by pressing `Ctrl`*
